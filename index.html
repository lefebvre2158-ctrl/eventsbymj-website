About Events by MJ

Hi, I'm Marijana (Maja) Bandjak

I'm an Edmonton-based event planner and balloon stylist with a passion for transforming ordinary spaces into extraordinary celebrations. Every event deserves that special touch that makes it truly memorable.

From elegant balloon stacks and whimsical arches to full-scale event styling, I bring creativity, precision, and heart to every project. Whether it's a birthday, baby shower, corporate event, or holiday celebration — I'm here to make your vision a reality.
