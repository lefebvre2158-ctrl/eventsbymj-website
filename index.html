Tips & Care

Balloon Care Guide

Get the most out of your balloon décor with these helpful care tips.

01

Avoid Direct Sunlight

UV rays and heat can cause balloons to oxidize, fade, and pop prematurely. Keep your décor in shaded or indoor areas for the longest life.

02

Temperature Matters

Extreme heat or cold can affect balloon size and float time. Room temperature (20-22°C) is ideal for maintaining their perfect shape.

03

Set Up Timing

For best results, balloon décor should be set up 1-3 hours before your event. We'll coordinate timing with you to ensure everything is picture-perfect.

04

Handle with Care

Avoid sharp objects, rough surfaces, and excessive handling. Treat your balloon décor gently and it will stay beautiful throughout your entire event.
